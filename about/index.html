On a snowy night in Torino, Italy, Dec. 6th 2020 AD (The covid years)

My hard-drive was almost full so I decided it was time to upload some of my photos somewhere. And here I am.
Before going digital I used a Nikon N6006 but, to be honest, my analogue photos are almost total rubbish.
I bought my first digital camera in 2006 (a D70s) and today I use a Nikon D800. My most precious lens is a Nikkor AF-S VR 300mm f2.8
I really like sport photography, especially MotoGP, especially Valentino Rossi.

Thank you for visiting my page, come back every now and then.
And please, judge tenderly of me.
